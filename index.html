Life According to Dad

An interview by Alex Requadt

.

Today, I have interviewed my dad, Scott Requadt.
Here are some of his responses!

What is your favorite food and why?

"My favorite type of food is steak because I like red meat."

What is your favorite music? Why do you like it?

"80's music because it was the music I listened to when I was in highschool... because it is the best music."

What is your favorite vacation place? Why do you have any specific memories that you can share?

"Our Christmas trip in Zermatt, I loved the day that we skied down into Italy for lunch, and then back into Switzerland for dinner."

What has been your greatest accomplishment in life

"Bringing up two well-balenced, thoughtful children."

What is one thing in the world that you would like to change?

"Hilary Clinton would be president."

What is one thing in the world the one thing in the world the makes you the most happy

"Spending time with my family!"
